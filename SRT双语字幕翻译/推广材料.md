# SRT双语字幕翻译 - 推广材料

## 应用描述

**中文描述：**
SRT双语字幕翻译是一款专为iPhone设计的字幕翻译工具。只需上传英文字幕SRT文件，即可快速生成双语字幕，支持预览和下载功能。无需联网，所有翻译处理都在设备本地完成，保护您的隐私安全。

**英文描述：**
SRT Bilingual Subtitle Translator is a specialized iPhone app for subtitle translation. Simply upload English SRT subtitle files and quickly generate bilingual subtitles with preview and download capabilities. No internet connection required - all translation processing is done locally on your device, ensuring privacy and security.
